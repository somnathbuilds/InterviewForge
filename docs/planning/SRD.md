# Project Description

InterviewForge is an AI-powered interview preparation platform that helps B.Tech, MCA, and freshers improve their technical skills through personalized interview practice, performance tracking, and AI-driven feedback.

---

# Problem Statement

Most students prepare for placements using multiple platforms for DSA, Core Subjects, HR interviews, and resume preparation. They often don't know their weak areas, cannot track their progress effectively, and lack personalized guidance for interview preparation.

---

# Objective

To build a single platform where students can practice technical interviews, receive AI-powered feedback, identify weak topics, track their progress, and become interview-ready with structured preparation.
