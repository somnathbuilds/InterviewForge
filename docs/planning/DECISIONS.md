## Decision 001

### Title

InterviewForge is not an AI Chatbot

### Decision

InterviewForge will be built as an AI-powered Placement Preparation Platform rather than a generic AI chatbot.

### Reason

AI is only one component of the system.

The primary value comes from:

- Structured learning
- Personalized roadmaps
- Progress tracking
- Company-specific preparation
- Analytics
- AI-powered guidance
