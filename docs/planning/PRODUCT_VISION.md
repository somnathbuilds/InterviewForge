# Product Vision

## 1. Mission
InterviewForge helps students maximize their placement potential through structured, strategic, and personalized preparation.

Instead of preparing randomly across multiple platforms, students receive a clear roadmap, track their progress, identify weak areas, and focus on what matters most for their target companies.

## Vision
To become the most trusted placement preparation platform for students by combining structured learning, data-driven progress tracking, and AI-powered guidance into one ecosystem.
